ABOUT ME

From as early as age seven, I found myself captivated by the world of visuals long before I even understood what “design” meant. I would walk through the streets reading every poster, billboard, and sign I saw, fascinated by how colors, shapes, and letters could speak without making a sound. That curiosity grew alongside my love for cartoons and the countless hours I spent drawing characters, logos, and anything my imagination could sketch onto paper.

As I grew older, that spark only burned brighter. I began experimenting with design on my mobile phone—creating simple flyers, editing photos, and trying to recreate the posters I admired. High school became the real turning point: I finally had access to the tools and devices needed to design more seriously. I started creating flyers for events, friends, and small groups, treating each project as a chance to learn something new. Since then, design has become more than a passion it’s a craft I constantly refine. I’ve completed over 100 free practice projects just to sharpen my skills, explore styles, and build range.

Along the way, I’ve also delivered 30+ real client and corporate projects, helping businesses communicate clearly, stand out visually, and build trust through intentional design. Today, I’m a Graphic Designer & Brand Identity Designer driven by the same sense of wonder I had at seven years old—but now equipped with experience, discipline, and a deep love for creating visuals that truly matter.
